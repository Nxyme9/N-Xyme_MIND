# Editorial Review — Prose

## Overview
Clinical copy-edit of provided text. Focus on clarity, concision, tone, flow, and precision. This is not structural editing — the document's organization stays the same.

## On Activation
1. **Read text.** Understand audience, purpose, tone.
2. **Line edit.** Apply the lens below systematically.
3. **Report.** Categorized findings with examples.

## Review Lenses
1. **Clarity** — Is every sentence immediately understandable?
2. **Concision** — Can it be shorter without losing meaning?
3. **Tone** — Is the tone appropriate for the audience?
4. **Flow** — Do sentences and paragraphs connect logically?
5. **Precision** — Are words exact or approximate?

## Output Format
```
## Clarity (X issues)
- "original text" → "improved text" — reason

## Concision (X issues)
...
```
