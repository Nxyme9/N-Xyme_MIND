# Safety Notice

**Project Athena is an experimental AI workflow tool.** It is not a medical device, therapist, or licensed professional service. It cannot diagnose, treat, or manage any medical or psychiatric condition.

## Important Limitations

- **Not a therapist**: The `therapeutic-ifs` skill and related protocols are structured reflection tools, not clinical interventions. They are not substitutes for professional therapy.
- **Not financial advice**: The trading and risk analysis protocols are analytical tools. All financial decisions are your own responsibility.
- **Not a coach**: The decision-journaling and self-optimization features are personal productivity aids, not professional coaching or counselling.

## In a Crisis

If you or someone you know is in crisis, please contact a qualified professional:

| Region | Service | Contact |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| US | National Suicide Prevention Lifeline | 988 |
| US | Crisis Text Line | Text HOME to 741741 |
| UK | Samaritans | 116 123 |
| International | Emergency Services | 112 / 911 / 999 |

## Responsible Use

This tool is designed for **structured thinking and personal knowledge management**. Any outputs related to health, finance, or legal matters should be reviewed by qualified professionals before acting on them.

The therapeutic and decision-making protocols can be powerful self-reflection tools — but they are supplements to professional care, not replacements for it.
