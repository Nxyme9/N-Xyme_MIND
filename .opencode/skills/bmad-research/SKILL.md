## What I am

Category gateway for **research** workflows.
I organize multiple sub-workflows so you pick only what you need.

## Sub-workflows

- **`bmad-domain-research`** — Industry and domain deep dive
- **`bmad-market-research`** — Competition and customer analysis
- **`bmad-technical-research`** — Technology and architecture research
- **`bmad-brainstorming`** — Creative ideation session

## How to use

1. Review the sub-workflows above
2. Identify which one matches the user's need
3. The full workflow instructions are in `bmad/` — read the relevant `workflow.md` file:
      - `bmad/.../bmad-domain-research/workflow.md`
   - `bmad/.../bmad-market-research/workflow.md`
   - `bmad/.../bmad-technical-research/workflow.md`
   - `bmad/.../bmad-brainstorming/workflow.md`
4. Follow the steps in that file
